C
Communication Communication skills are crucial in the ICT field. Students learn how to communicate ideas, data, and information effectively using various digital platforms and tools. This includes written communication, verbal communication, and digital communication methods such as email, messaging, and social media.
I
Information In the ICT strand, students learn how to effectively gather, process, manage, and utilize information. This includes understanding how to access information from various sources, critically evaluate its credibility and relevance, and organize it for efficient use.
T
Technology The 'T' in ICT refers to technology. Students in the ICT strand study various technologies, including hardware, software, networks, and digital systems. They learn how to use technology tools and applications to solve problems, create solutions, and innovate in different contexts.
